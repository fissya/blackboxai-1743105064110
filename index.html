About FEFO

FEFO (Fantastic Event For Owls) is an annual gathering that celebrates the wisdom and beauty of owls through various activities and competitions.

This year marks our 9th edition, promising to be the most spectacular yet with new events, guest speakers, and interactive exhibits.

The event will take place on November 20, 2025 at the Grand Owl Convention Center.
